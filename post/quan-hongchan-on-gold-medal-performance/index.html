东京奥运速递｜完美：满分，满分，满分！

悦读
2021-08-07
英语

本届奥运中国代表团年龄最小的运动员全红婵以三个满分动作强势获得跳水女子10米台的冠军。
看奥运学英语。今天关于全红婵完美表现的介绍摘自 CNN 网站报道 “I was a little nervous”: 14-year-old diving sensation Quan Hongchan on gold medal performance 。

The concerns of most 14-year-old’s typically stretch from school to social encounters.
大多数14岁的孩子通常关心的是学业和各种社交活动。

Not for Quan Hongchan though.
全红婵却不是这样。

The 14-year-old diving sensation①—China’s youngest athlete at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics—won a stunning② gold medal in the women’s 10 meter platform diving on Thursday.
在8月5日的跳水女子10米跳台决赛中，这位14岁的跳水新星一鸣惊人，凭借令人惊艳的表现赢得了金牌。她是2020东京奥运会中国代表团最年轻的运动员。

Quan becomes the second-youngest woman ever to win gold in the event after her compatriot Fu Mingxia took the title at the 1992 Barcelona Games at the age of just 13.
中国选手伏明霞1992年在巴塞罗那奥运会夺金时年仅13岁，全红婵如今成为继伏明霞之后跳水项目有史以来第二年轻的女性金牌得主。

The Asian nation has now claimed③ gold in all women’s diving events at four successive④ Games—Beijing 2008, London 2012, Rio 2016 and Tokyo 2020.
中国现已连续四届奥运会——2008北京、2012伦敦、2016里约和2020东京——包揽所有女子跳水项目金牌。

And although she’s not getting ahead of herself about competing at Paris 2024, Quan already has an idea about how she wants to celebrate.
全红婵还没开始为参战2024年巴黎奥运会做准备，但她已经想好了这次的庆祝方式。

“I want to eat a lot of delicious things tonight! I feel like eating latiao (a popular Chinese spicy snack) the most.”
“今天晚上我要吃很多好吃的东西！现在我特别想吃辣条（风靡中国的一种麻辣味零食）。”

【注释】

① sensation: /sen’seɪʃ(ə)n/ n. very great surprise, excitement, or interest among a lot of people; the person or the thing that causes this surprise 引起轰动的人（或事物）
② stunning: /‘stʌnɪŋ/ adj. extremely attractive or impressive 极有魅力的；给人深刻印象的
③ claim: /kleɪm/ v. to gain, win or achieve sth 获得；赢得
④ successive: /sək’sesɪv/ adj. following immediately one after the other 连续的；接连的
