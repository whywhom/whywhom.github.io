Iron Flame by Rebecca Yarros

Goodreads 2023年11月最流行书籍Top10

  • December 13, 2023
  • 2 min read

ISBN号:09781649374172 书名:Iron Flame 作者:Rebecca Yarros

英文简介:

“The first year is when some of us lose our lives. The second year is when the rest of us lose our humanity.” –Xaden Riorson

Everyone expected Violet Sorrengail to die during her first year at Basgiath War College–Violet included. But Threshing was only the first impossible test meant to weed out the weak-willed, the unworthy, and the unlucky.

Now the real training begins, and Violet’s already wondering how she’ll get through. It’s not just that it’s grueling and maliciously brutal, or even that it’s designed to stretch the riders’ capacity for pain beyond endurance. It’s the new vice commandant, who’s made it his personal mission to teach Violet exactly how powerless she is-unless she betrays the man she loves.

Although Violet’s body might be weaker and frailer than everyone else’s, she still has her wits–and a will of iron. And leadership is forgetting the most important lesson Basgiath has taught her: Dragon riders make their own rules.

But a determination to survive won’t be enough this year.

Because Violet knows the real secret hidden for centuries at Basgiath War College–and nothing, not even dragon fire, may be enough to save them in the end.

The Empyrean series is best enjoyed in order. Reading Order: Book #1 Fourth Wing Book #2 Iron Flame

中文简介:

“第一年，我们中的一些人失去了生命。第二年，我们其他人失去了人性。” ——哈登·里尔森

每个人都认为维奥莱特·索伦盖尔会在巴斯吉斯战争学院的第一年死去——包括维奥莱特。 但脱粒只是第一个不可能的考验，旨在淘汰那些意志薄弱、不值得和不幸的人。

现在真正的训练开始了，维奥莱特已经在想她将如何度过。 这不仅是因为它令人筋疲力尽和恶意残酷，甚至它的设计初衷是为了让骑手承受超出忍耐的疼痛能力。 新任副司令以让维奥莱特明白她是多么无能为力为己任，除非她背叛她所爱的男人。

尽管薇尔莉特的身体可能比其他人都要虚弱，但她仍然有她的智慧——和钢铁般的意志。 而领导层却忘记了巴斯吉亚斯教给她的最重要的一课：龙骑士制定自己的规则。

但今年光有生存的决心还不够。

因为维奥莱特知道巴斯吉斯战争学院隐藏了几个世纪的真正秘密——而没有任何东西，甚至龙火，最终可能足以拯救他们。

《九重天》系列最好按顺序欣赏。
